Message

From CEO

Fassil Zewde Dinku

CEO

It is my greatest pleasure to welcome you to Gesh Abay Import and Export PLC

It seems that our establishment in 2007 was just yesterday, but time has passed by quickly as much as the changing world. Since the commencement of exporting coffee beans, Oil seeds, Beans and Pulses to different parts of the world and importing various products to Ethiopia, we have repeated the rapid growth of 30% or more and were awarded best exportation certificates from governmental and giant national corporations like banks.

Most notably, the country’s resilience and struggle to recover the financial crisis and foreign currency shortage reflects our ability to recognize opportunities in the global market. As you navigate our website I hope you learn more about the qualities that make our company an outstanding import and Export Company and a wonderful place to build competence and expertise.

In Gesh Abay we live our values; our values define how we operate every single day - Honesty, Integrity, Fairness, Professionalism, excellence and quality in our products. They reflect how we interact with our associates, clients and the community. We hold each other responsible for creating a company we all can be proud to work at. Whether you are a potential customer, a small business partner or a future employee we look forward to finding out how we can work together to export and import quality and affordable products to the public.

Gesh Abay has always been at the forefront in assessing problems of the society and contributing in solving them and acting as a platform for expanding business activities between companies at home and abroad. We make progressive transformation in people’s life by assessing what the community needs critically as in the case of the pharmaceutical import division we started recently. We have a client first philosophy and for this very reason, we have a true international reach for all products that we export and import.

Gesh Abay has earned the trust and respect of international and local clients for one simple reason- we have great people. They are experts in their fields. They take ownership and vanity in the jobs they do. We bring passion, immodesty and experience together.

“ሎሌ መስሎ ቢሰሩ ጌታ መስሎ ይበሉ” (“Grind as a Servant, Reap the Benefits as a Lord”) is a proverbial phrase that resonates with Ethiopia’s history of turning crises into opportunities.

Thank You!!!

Fassil Zewde Dinku, CEO
