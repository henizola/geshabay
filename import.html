The company has been engaged in importing a wide variety of items. Our primary goal is to bridge the gap in the scarcity and high cost of goods in Ethiopia. Some of the products that we import include:

Steel Sheet

Sheet metal is used in the outer bodies of automobiles and trucks, furniture and construction, medical tables, roofs for buildings, etc

Reinforcing Bar

Rebar (short for reinforcing bar), known when massed as reinforcing steel or reinforcement steel is a steel bar or mesh of steel wires used as a tension device in reinforced concrete and reinforced masonry structures to strengthen and aid the concrete under tension.

Timber

These are used in construction work (timber-framed buildings), fencing, pallets and furniture floorboards.

Tires

Mostly for light and heavy trucks; our company also imports tires that are fitted for automobiles.

Sino Truck

Gesh Abay imports durable and sustainable Sinotrucks for construction and cargo uses.

Pharmaceutical Item

Close observation has aided us in assessing the problems in our country. As members of the society, we are addressing and contributing to problems that will benefit humanity at large. Our company has begun to import high-quality pharmaceuticals, different chemicals, medical supplies and devices at an affordable price to meet the needs and demands of consumers in Ethiopia.
