About

History

Our company’s journey in the industry commenced more than a decade ago with steel fabrication. Our insight was that foreign currency exchange is a phenomenon that needs to be addressed and requires more solution. Thus, the journey which started in 2007 G.C. expanded and made us establish more complimentary businesses. Today, the company has evolved into a corporate group with over a hundred employees. Gesh Abay has modern coffee cleaning and storage facilities lying on 4,500 square meters with a processing capacity of 2.5 coffee tons per hour. Our company is lauded for exporting beans, oilseeds, specialty and conventional coffee all over the world. Different companies in the Middle East, Asia, Europe and America are working closely with us.

Mame Steel Mill PLC

Established in 2007 G.C, Mame Steel Mill PLC is modern and well-equipped steel manufacturing factory; using advanced Italian machineries in Addis Ababa, Akaki Kality. The factory has a commendable production capacity of 100,000 tons per annum. Our flagship products are:

Square Hollow Section (SHS)

Square Hollow Section, as the name suggests, is square steel with a hollow core made of black steel sheet, providing a surface with less risk of cracking on the edges.

Most Suitable for Furniture and Construction Purposes

Rectangular Hollow Section (RHS)

Rectangular hollow sections (RHS) are a popular choice for mechanical, structural and construction applications. Due to the rectangular shape of this hollow section type, when joining to other flat surfaces, sections only need to be straight cut. RHS requires minimal edge preparation for welding or joining.

Most Suitable for Furniture and Construction Purposes

Circular Hollow Section (CHS)

Circular hollow sections, often known as round steel tubes, are a popular type of steel section used in a range of sectors in South Africa. These steel pieces are made from rolled steel sheets or slit coils. A variety of materials can be used to roll around steel parts.

Most Suitable for Furniture and Construction Purposes
