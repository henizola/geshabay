Export

Gesh Abay Import Export PLC operates a modern coffee cleaning and storage facility that is built on 4500 square meters in Addis Ababa. It has a processing capability of 2.5 tons per hour. With an on-site coffee laboratory; the company carries out testing procedures with its own personnel and protocols. The warehouses are clean and well-lit, with an efficient ventilation system.

Coffee beans

Ethiopia is the birthplace of Coffee Arabica. The country’s natural gift with an appropriate soil type, high altitude, temperature and PH is the secret behind it. This makes a fantastic combination to acquire world-class and famous coffee. Gesh Abay, in turn, is well-known as a global supplier of high-quality organic coffee in a wide range of flavors.
